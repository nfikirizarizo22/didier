About Me

I'm a passionate student who finds beauty in both the mathematical precision of science and the emotional expression of music.

My Journey

Currently in my fifth year of secondary education (S5) studying PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) at Liquidnet Family High School in Agahozo Shalom Youth Village. I'm dedicated to understanding the intricate workings of our natural world while expressing my creativity through piano.

My dual passion for science and music has taught me that both require discipline, practice, and an appreciation for patterns and beauty.
